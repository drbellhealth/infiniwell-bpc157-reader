Active Ingredients

Here is what the capsule holds, plus the one delivery detail that matters most:

Other Ingredients (Excipients)

Because Infiniwell sells the peptide by itself, there is hardly any 'other stuff' to talk about — a vegetarian capsule shell and not much more, no blend, no marketing fillers. The thing that actually matters is not excipients, it is how you store it. BPC-157 is a peptide, which makes it heat-sensitive: leave it in a hot car or beside the stove and it can break down, so keep it cool. Your current label is the final word on exactly what is in the capsule.

Allergens and Sensitivities

As a single-peptide capsule in a vegetarian shell, this does not carry the usual allergy concerns — there is no shellfish-derived glucosamine, no dairy, and no herbal blend to react to. The honest 'heads-up' for a peptide is different: it is a synthetic compound that has not gone through the human safety testing a real drug would, so the cautions that count are about your situation (cancer, pregnancy, immune-suppressing drugs) rather than a typical allergy warning. If capsule ingredients tend to bother you, still read the label.

How Ingredients Compare to Similar Products

Two comparisons matter most. First, capsule versus injection: nearly all the well-known research used injections, which come through doctors and compounding pharmacies, while Infiniwell's capsule is the easier, lower-barrier choice whose best-supported effect is in the gut. If your goal is gut-related, the oral route lines up well; if you are after a far-off tendon or ligament effect, the capsule is the weaker bet and the evidence is thin. Second, single peptide versus a 'recovery blend': Infiniwell sells BPC-157 on its own instead of hiding it in a proprietary mix, so the dose is right there in the open, which is usually the better value. And it is worth being clear about what it is not — not a proven drug, not a replacement for rehab, and not a daily forever-supplement; it is a short experiment that works best attached to a real recovery plan.
